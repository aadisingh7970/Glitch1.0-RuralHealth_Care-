About Us

Welcome to the Doctor’s Appointment Prep Tool! Our mission is to empower patients to make the most of their healthcare visits. We understand that remembering symptoms, medications, and questions during a consultation can be challenging.

Our web app helps you prepare for your doctor’s appointment by providing a simple way to record your symptoms, medications, and any questions you want to ask. You can easily generate a summary and download it as a PDF to take with you.

We believe in accessible, patient-centered care. No login is required, and your privacy is respected. Thank you for using our tool to improve your healthcare experience!
